Principles

$DEVOPS isn’t just using a tech buzzword — the project is structured around real DevOps principles that bring stability, speed, and culture to the memecoin world.

  • 🚀 Continuous Integration & Continuous Delivery (CI/CD): Frequent, incremental improvements with fast feedback loops — moving ideas from concept to production quickly.
  • 🤖 Automation First: Manual work is minimized through bots, scripts, and workflows that handle deployments, updates, and operational tasks.
  • 🧠 Infrastructure as Code: Everything — from the website to pipelines — is treated like code: versioned, transparent, and reproducible.
  • 🌐 Observability & Feedback Loops: Just like resilient systems, the project tracks signals and responds early to keep direction aligned with community needs.
  • 🧰 Continuous Improvement & Iteration: The culture embraces learning and evolving quickly, releasing early and often.
  • 🤝 Collaboration & Transparency: Breaking down silos between builders, marketers, and meme creators through open repos, shared discussions, and community ownership.
  • 🧱 Resilience Through Culture: Memecoins are chaotic — DevOps culture provides the scaffolding to adapt, automate, and thrive under pressure.

Tokenomics

$DEVOPS has officially launched on pump.fun with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. All tokens are distributed on-chain through the bonding curve — no presale, no VC allocations, and no hidden minting.

  • 🪙 Fixed Supply: 1 Billion $DEVOPS — no future minting.
  • 🌐 Launch Platform: pump.fun bonding curve — fully transparent, on-chain distribution.
  • 🧠 Stealth Builder Phase: Live token, but no public promotion yet — early builders can establish positions.
  • 🧑‍💻 Early Builder Participation: Access through awareness, not private deals or whitelists.
  • 🚫 No VC / No Team Allocation: No private investors, no lockups.
  • 🔒 Liquidity: Auto-paired and locked by pump.fun at bonding curve graduation.
  • ⛓ Token Standard: SPL Token on Solana.
